Shareholder Perk Calculator
ℹ️ How this works

This tool helps you figure out whether a shareholder membership is worth the money. You list all the perks (benefits) included in the membership, estimate how much each one is worth and how often it gets used, and the calculator tells you whether the total value exceeds what you're paying.

The final score is called the SVS (Shareholder Value Score). An SVS above 1.0 means you're getting more value than you're paying — anything above 1.5 is exceptional.

Partner ROI & Fairness Calculator
ℹ️ How this works

On the partner side, IB gives money and the partner gives perks or value back to IB. You enter the contract amount IB is paying, then list every perk the partner gives IB so the calculator can total the return. Unlimited perks use the IB leverage factor below.

IB ROI Score — Total perk value to IB ÷ contract amount given by IB. Above 1.0 means IB is getting back more value than it pays.

Fairness Score — Contract amount given by IB ÷ total perk value to IB. A score near 1.0 is the fairest range for both sides.

Is IB getting enough perk value back for the money it gives?

Fairness Score ?Measures whether the money IB gives stays balanced against the perk value IB gets. Fairness Score = Contract Amount Given by IB ÷ Total Perk Value to IB.
Is the money IB gives balanced fairly against the partner perks IB gets?
—
Fairness = Contract Amount Given ÷ Total Perk Value to IB
Below 0.85 — Partner Light: The partner may be giving IB more perk value than the cash they receive.
0.85–1.15 — Balanced: The money given and perk value received are in a fair range.
Above 1.15 — IB Heavy Give: IB may be giving more money than the partner perks are worth.
